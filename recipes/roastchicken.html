Roast Chicken

A super easy meal by me!

This requires very little work and goes great with rice (recipe not included) and broccoli

To prepare this savoury dish, you will need:

  • 5 Chicken breasts
  • Salt,to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • 125ml lemon juice
  • 250ml olive oil

Now let's put it all together:

  1. Cut chicken breast in halves and place on tray lined with tin foil.
  2. Dice your onion and put aside.
  3. Season the surface of the chicken with salt and pepper. flip to other side and repeat.
  4. Sprinkle onion evenly over the chicken breasts.
  5. Drizzle the olive oil on the chicken breasts.
  6. Heat oven to 370 degrees and place tray inside oven for 40 minutes.
  7. Remove tray from oven and flip chicken.
  8. Sprinkle lemon juice over the chicken.
  9. Place tray in oven again for another 15 minutes.
  10. Remove tray from oven and let rest. Then serve.
