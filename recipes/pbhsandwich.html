Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich +

Quick and awesome breakfast!

It might sound simple, but there's a twist

To prepare this Sandwich, you will need:

  • 2 slices of 100% whole grain toast.
  • 1Tbsp of peanut butter.
  • 2 Tbsp of honey.
  • Half a banana.
  • A pinch of cinnamon.

Now let's put it all together:

  1. place the first slice of toast on your plate and spread peanut butter.
  2. cut banana into slices and place on peanut butter.
  3. sprinkle cinnamon on top.
  4. pour honey over everything.
  5. Add second slice to wrap it up.
